Open. Nonproft. Cute ♥

Chat with your friends from Ubuntu Touch.

Open
Opensource and open development where everyone can join.

Nonprofit
Fluffychat is donations funded.

Cute ♥
Cute design and much theme settings including a dark mode.

Direct- and groupchats
Unlimited groups and direct chats.

Easy
Fluffychat is made as simple to use as possible.

Free
Free to use for everyone without ads.

Connective
Fluffychat can use your addressbook to find your friends.

Decentralized
There is no „fluffychat server“ which can track you.

Compatible
Compatible with Riot, Fractal, Nekho and all matrix messengers.

Fluffychat comes with a dream

Imagine a world where everyone can choose the messenger he likes and is still able to chat with all of his/her friends. A world where is no single company which tracks all selfies you send to your love. And a world where apps are made for fluffyness and not for profit. ♥
